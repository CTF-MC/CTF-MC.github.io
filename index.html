Capture the Flag

What is Capture the Flag?

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a multiplayer minigame where players compete to capture the opposing team's flag while defending their own. The game encourages teamwork, strategy, and quick thinking.

How to Play

  1. Join a server that hosts CTF games.
  2. Choose your team and get ready to play.
  3. Work with your teammates to capture the enemy's flag.
  4. Defend your own flag from being captured.
  5. The first team to capture the opponent's flag wins!

Features

  • Multiple maps with unique challenges
  • Customizable teams and flags
  • Supports up to 16 players per game
  • Regular updates with new content

Classes

Capture the Flag features various classes that players can choose from, each with unique abilities and roles:

Beast Tamer

The Beast Tamer is a class that excels at controlling the battlefield with their animal companions and unique items.

Guardian

The Guardian is a steadfast defender, equipped with heavy armor and tools to protect allies and hold the line.

Ranger

The Ranger is a class that excels at striking from a distance with precision and agility, using their mastery of the wild to outmaneuver enemies and track targets across any terrain.

Rogue

The Rogue is a master of ambush and evasion, choosing to hide in the shadows and wait for the opportune moment to strike instead of charging into battle head-first.

Spear Thrower

The Spear Thrower is a ranged warrior that fights with deadly precision and high-impact tools.

Swordsman

The Swordsman is a balanced warrior equipped with close-combat and ranged tools, capable of high burst damage.

Wizard

The Wizard is a master of spellcasting, using powerful magical items to control the battlefield.
